🡹 ATEEZ' recent album title track's music video: INCEPTION

ATEEZ is an eight-member K-POP (Korean Pop) boy band. These talented boys debuted on the 24st of October, 2018 with two title tracks: “Treasure” and “Pirate King”.

They were recruited by KQ Entertainment, which was – honestly still is – a small company. They’re a 4th generation K-POP group and they weren’t popular in the beginning at all.

Before their debut, these men were known as “KQ Fellaz”. When they debuted, they were no longer “KQ Fellaz” but “ATEEZ”. Despite not getting much recognition at their debut, they are one of the most popular and successful 4th generation K-POP groups today.

Only a few months after their debut, a little after their second album was published, they sold out an entire world tour and even hosted a few extra concerts because all the tickets got sold out in no-time.

They have already won a few music awards, of which some being very important awards, and they definitely deserve it. There is nothing ATEEZ can’t do. Their dance, vocal, rap, visual, performance and personalities are outstanding.
