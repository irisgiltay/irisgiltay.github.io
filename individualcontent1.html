Besides ATEEZ' usual content as a whole group, they also have their very own indiviual content!
In this video the boys revealed what kind of content each individual member would make a series of.
These are the names of their own individual content series:
  • BY.HONGJOONG
  • MARS ASMR
  • ANEWZ
  • Yeosang's Omniscient View
  • Gourmet San: the Sanchelin Guide
  • Minkiway
  • Enjoy~ Muk-Wooyoung
  • JjongST

BY.HONGJOONG

Hongjoong:
"My content's title is 'BY.HONGJOONG'
Actually, when I started doing this content at the beginning, I did it because I wanted to introduce some old songs.
But it would be great if I can not only show the songs, but also how I make them. I want to show something different through 'BY.HONGJOONG'
So I upgraded 'BY.HONGJOONG' widely."

In 'BY.HONGJOONG' Hongjoong covers old songs from his favourite idols. He arranges and sings a whole new version himself.
The content name says it all: everything is done by Hongjoong himself!
Two of his covers contain a 'behind the scenes' clip to show everyone the process behind the making of the cover and the music video.

MARS ASMR

Seonghwa:
"My content is 'MARS ASMR'
It just means I'll make clips that contain sounds of various things.
I want to make something interesting and funny, or clips of me finding sounds, and let you listen to sounds that make you feel comfortable.
It's one of my purposes too, so I just hope you can enjoy it."

In 'MARS ASMR' Seonghwa makes ASMR in an original way.
For example: in the first video of this series, he had to complete ASMR-related challenges. His second video was royalty-themed and his third video is office-themed.
The meaning behind 'MARS ASMR' is simple.
ASMR is the content Seonghwa makes and MARS is one of Seonghwa's nickname (his name backwards, Hwaseong, means 'Mars' in Korea)
And to top that all off, MARS has the same letters as ASMR.

His ASMR videos:

MARS ASMR #3 - 회사원 성화의 하루 (A day at the office with Seonghwa): 136k views (posted: December 22nd, 2020)

ANEWZ

Yunho:
"My content's title is 'ANEWZ'
Through ANEWZ - for ATINY, not only myself - I want to show our members as well.
I want to try to make something that ATINY could be interested in.

Yunho has already been making 'ANEWZ' videos for a while before they decided to make individual content, so he just decided to continue with it!
ANEWZ is basically a news program in which Yunho shares some TMI about the members or other interesting things such as future plans for the group.
Now, Yunho has started to interview members as well and include the members more in 'ANEWZ'

Yeosang's Omniscient View

Yeosang:
"My content consists of me doing what our fans have always wanted to do, and giving them my own reviews about it!
Our fans who don't have time or are too busy for leisure, can kind of experience these things virtually."

In 'Yeosang's Omniscient View' Yeosang does several fun, random experiments that you would likely only do when you're really bored.
After doing these experiments and/or challenges, he reviews them.

Gourmet San: the Sanchelin Guide

San:
"My content's title is 'Gourmet San: the Sanchelin Guide'
What I want to show is myself enjoying food.
I want to make you happy.
When I taste the food, I want to feel like 'I hope ATINY can try these delicious foods'"

In 'Gourmet San: the Sanchelin Guide' San visits some Korean restaurants to try their food.
As he eats, he will give his opinion and most likely recommend the place to the fans.

Minkiway

Mingi:
"My content's title is 'Minkiway'
The reason why I chose this content is that I thought I could show what I'm interested in.
I'm so excited, because I can show different content from me, not what I've shown until now.
I'm preparing a lot of things, actually.
I'll try to make something interesting, that you would say 'Wow, even this?'"

In 'Minkiway' Mingi will show the fans what his taste in fashion is like.
He shows the way he combines his clothes and the way they create a certain looks, giving fashion tips along the way.
He chose for the name 'Minkyway' because it's a combination of Mingi's nickname 'Minki' and the word 'runway'.
The funny thing is that it kind of sounds like Milkyway.

Mingi's fashion videos:
밍키웨이 #1 – 상황 별 꾸안꾸 스타일링 팁 (Quanku styling tips for different places): 270k views (posted: May 1st. 2020)

Enjoy~ Muk-Wooyoung

Wooyoung:
"My content's title is 'Enjoy~ Muk-Wooyoung'
I'm so happy that I became a singer with ATEEZ' members, which was my first dream.
And my second dream is to get a cooking certificate in Korean cuisine, so I've decided to film 'Enjoy~ Muk-Wooyoung'
I want to show how I seriously enjoy cooking and how my cooking skills improve."

Wooyoung's content 'Enjoy~ Muk-Wooyoung' is a series in which Wooyoung learns how to cook, cooks some dishes himself and tries them.
Sometimes he gets to obtain cooking skills from famous Korean chefs!
'Muk-Wooyoung' is a combination of 'mukbang' and 'Wooyoung' which is his own name.

JjongST

Jongho:
"My content's title is 'JjongST'
In this series I will go to the k-dramas' locations and recommend different OST (Original Soundtracks) from dramas I enjoyed watching.
Even though I'm the main vocalist of ATEEZ, I haven't tried a content about songs yet.
Through this, I hope you can feel ATEEZ is good at singing too, not only at performing.
Please look forward to it!"

In 'JjongST' Jongho goes to the locations where some of his favourite dramas were filmed to cover their OST.
The name 'JjongST' is a combination of 'Jjong' - Jongho's nickname - and 'OST'.

EXTRA : ATEEZ - BLACK CAT NERO

ATEEZ covered TURBO's 'Black Cat Nero' and performed with this song at 'Immortal Songs' (am important Korean singing contest).
Fun fact: they actually won!
Just like Hongjoong rearranges and covers songs, the whole group did the same for this song.
The fans liked it so much that on Halloween, they released a MV (music video) for their cover on their channel which is Halloween-themed.
This cover is one of the most iconic covers ATEEZ has ever done and I have to admit that the performance and the video were both outstanding!
